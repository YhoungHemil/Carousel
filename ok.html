What is PortaFlux? PortaFlux is an all-in-one digital platform designed to inform, entertain, and empower users with a seamless experience across news, movies, tech updates, and smart digital content. Whether you're browsing the latest headlines, streaming trending videos, or exploring tech insights, PortaFlux delivers curated, quality content,right when you need it. It’s not just a content hub, it’s your daily destination for staying connected, learning something new, and discovering what matters. Key Features of PortaFlux: 1. Daily News Feed Real-time news updates Global and local stories Business, politics, entertainment, and tech categories Clean, easy-to-read layout 2. Live TV & Video Streaming Watch live news channels from around the world Stream trending and curated videos Integrated support for free and premium channels 3. Movie & Series Library Discover and stream popular movies and shows Categorized by genre, region, and latest releases 3. Tech Updates & Trends Read the latest in gadgets, apps, AI, and digital innovations Bite-sized articles and explainers Weekly tech highlights 5. Playlist & Category Browsing Organize videos into playlists Filter by category (News, Movies, Tech, etc.) Search and discover with ease 6. Token-Based Premium Access Subscribe to unlock premium content Secure token access via email Admin-controlled single-use tokens for secure entry 7. Commenting, Likes & Dislikes (Social Interaction) Engage with content through reactions Post and read comments Moderated to ensure quality and safety (powered by Firebase) 8. Subscription & Payment Integration payment system Weekly, monthly, and yearly plans Email receipt + token generation for premium access 9. Responsive, User-Friendly Design Mobile-first, fast-loading interface Smooth navigation across devices Minimalist and modern aesthetic 10. Secure & Scalable Hosted via Netlify with serverless backend functions Firebase for real-time content interaction Fully encrypted token access for premium videos
